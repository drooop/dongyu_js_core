Thesis:
Wantai Network creates the business channel for AI to enter industrial services by converting AI intent, field capability, workstation service, delivery acceptance, audit records, and settlement basis into the ODS order delivery system.

Audience:
Industrial investors,产业基金,government industry departments, strategic partners.

One-line arc:
AI cannot directly control factories; it needs a business network, field decision boundary, and order-delivery economy that turns model intent into auditable industrial operations.
