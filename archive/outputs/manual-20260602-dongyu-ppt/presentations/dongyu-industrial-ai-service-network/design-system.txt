Design name: Industrial Strategy Brief

Slide size:
- 1280 x 720 px, widescreen.

Background system:
- Main background: #F7F8F6 cold paper.
- Content surfaces: #FFFFFF only when a real grouping is required.
- Fine separators: #D9DEE2 hairlines.
- No gradients, glows, bokeh, or decorative texture.

Typography:
- Title / claims: Noto Sans CJK SC / PingFang SC fallback, semibold.
- Labels / body: Noto Sans CJK SC / PingFang SC fallback, regular.
- Numbers and English: IBM Plex Sans / Inter fallback.
- Claim title: 34-46 px.
- Cover title: 52-66 px.
- Body: 18-23 px.
- Table labels: 13-16 px.
- Footer/source: 9-11 px.

Color palette:
- Ink: #18212F for primary text.
- Steel blue: #234E5F for chapter / structural emphasis.
- Oxidized teal: #0B7774 for ODS order delivery, channel, flow, traffic.
- Dark copper: #B36B2C for investment, asset formation, audit evidence.
- Oxide red: #9B3F36 for risk / blocked execution.
- Cool gray: #657180 for secondary labels.
- Line gray: #D9DEE2.
- Mist gray: #EBEFF2.

Color use:
- 75% white/cold paper, 15% ink/gray, 7% steel blue/teal, 3% copper/red.
- Teal means order delivery flow or ODS traffic.
- Copper means capital, asset, audit, or conversion to value.
- Red only means risk/intercept/non-execution.

Chart grammar:
- Prefer editable shape charts with direct labels.
- No heavy legends when direct labels work.
- Use hairline axes and small tick labels only when needed.
- Numbers must be sourced from BP or clearly labeled as planning target.

Diagram grammar:
- Rectangles and lanes encode systems/stages; thin connectors encode actual flow.
- Arrows only when direction matters.
- Equal-role boxes share exact dimensions and padding.
- Network nodes are small numbered squares or dots, never glowing circles.

Container grammar:
- Do not put cards inside cards.
- Do not wrap prose in boxes unless it is a metric, asset, stage, or table cell.
- Border radius: 0 to 4 px only.
- Shadows are forbidden.

Footer/page marker:
- Left footer: source note where needed.
- Right marker: two-digit slide number.
- Footer text #657180 at 10 px.

Kicker grammar:
- Upper-left small label, uppercase English or concise Chinese.
- Kicker marker is a 12 px horizontal rule in slide accent color; text vertically centered with marker.

Allowed layout families:
- Cover with metric rail.
- Bridge diagram.
- Transformation table.
- Architecture split.
- Operating loop.
- Matrix / route map.
- Funding-to-asset allocation.
- Closing ladder.

Banned motifs:
- AI brain, robot silhouette, chip circuit background, purple-blue gradient, glassmorphism, glowing nodes, decorative orbit lines, default rounded SaaS cards, fake logos, pseudo product UI.
