Reference audit:

Siemens Industrial Copilot:
- Useful: industrial AI is described through human-machine collaboration, automation code, simulation, maintenance assistance, and data control.
- PPT implication: do not sell "AI magic"; show controlled workflows and domain boundaries.

WEF Global Lighthouse Network:
- Useful: the credible manufacturing story is pilot to scale, replication, network learning, and proven use cases.
- PPT implication: roadmap slides should emphasize sample-to-replication-to-network, not a pure technology roadmap.

Schneider Electric EcoStruxure:
- Useful: platform story connects shop floor, sensors, cloud, analytics, and business logic.
- PPT implication: Wantai Network can be shown as business network between field execution and cloud intelligence.

IBM / Carbon:
- Useful: enterprise palette, contrast, data palette sequence, strong neutrals plus deliberate accent.
- PPT implication: use restrained industrial colors and avoid AI-purple templates.

Anti-patterns to avoid:
- Generic SaaS dashboard card grid.
- AI purple-blue gradients.
- Brain / robot / chip imagery.
- Decorative arrows that do not carry business logic.
- Investor slides that read as internal Q&A.
